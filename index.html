2024 Solution Challenge

The mission of the Solution Challenge is to solve for one or more of the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals using Google technology. Since 2020, Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) members from around the world come together to create innovative solutions to tackle some of the world's most pressing challenges. Solution Challenge is open to GDSC members from universities all over. Join a GDSC to learn more.
